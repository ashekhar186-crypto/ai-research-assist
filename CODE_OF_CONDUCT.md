# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of positive behavior:

- giving thoughtful, actionable feedback
- assuming good intent
- keeping discussions focused on the work
- welcoming questions from contributors at different experience levels

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- hostile or intentionally dismissive communication
- publishing private information without permission
- disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do not align with this standard.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainer through GitHub.
